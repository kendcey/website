Marriott International

Research Business Analysis Web Report 2024
View Live

Description

This project presents a comprehensive web-based report on Marriott International, focusing on an external environment analysis for the end of 2024. The site was created to make the research accessible, interactive, and visually engaging for readers interested in business strategy and hospitality industry trends.

  • Purpose: To examine Marriott International's external environment and strategic position at the close of 2024.
  • Features: PESTEL analysis, industry trends, and strategic recommendations.
  • Format: Clean, web-based presentation for easy navigation and readability.
  • Tech: HTML5, CSS3, Responsive Design.
