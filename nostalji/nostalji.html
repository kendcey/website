Nostalji

Clothing Design Branding Identity

Overview

I developed the complete brand identity for Nostalji, a startup clothing brand founded by a Haitian American entrepreneur from Chicago. This project encompassed logo design, apparel design, and graphic design across multiple product lines. "Nostalji" is Haitian Creole for "nostalgia," reflecting the brand's cultural heritage and emotional connection to the past.
